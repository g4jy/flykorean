Philosophy

Three pillars.

Pronunciation First

Even with 10 words, correct pronunciation makes Koreans understand you. We start here because Korean is forgiving on grammar but unforgiving on sounds.

가Plain — soft
카Aspirated — air
까Tensed — tight

Sentence Logic

Understand why Korean works the way it does. Create your own sentences, not textbook phrases.

English: action first

I bought a bag in Paris

Korean: context first

저는 파리에서 가방을 샀어요

Cultural Context

The same phrase can be polite or rude. 7 formality levels. I teach you to read the room, not just the textbook.

고마워friends
감사해요polite
감사합니다formal
감사드립니다very formal
