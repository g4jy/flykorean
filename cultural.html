My Story · 나의 이야기

Why I Teach Korean

In March 2025, I walked away from my business. I was completely burnt out. For the first time in my life, I started traveling. As I wandered through different countries, I met incredible people genuinely curious about Korea.

"Why do Koreans always ask about age?" — 왜 나이를 물어봐요?

"Why does the verb come at the very end?" — 왜 동사가 마지막에?

"Why do you write addresses backwards?" — 왜 주소가 거꾸로?

Before teaching Korean, I ran a coaching center in Korea for four years. I was ranked 170th out of 450,000 tutors — the top 0.03%.

Philosophy · 교육 철학

Three Pillars of Mastery

Pronunciation First

발음이 먼저

Even with 10 words, correct pronunciation makes Koreans understand you. Korean has three consonant levels that English doesn't have.

가 Plain — soft, relaxed
카 Aspirated — strong air
까 Tensed — tight, no air

Sentence Logic

문장 구조의 논리

Understand why Korean sentences work the way they do. Create your own sentences, not just textbook phrases.

English: action first

I bought a bag in Paris

Korean: context first

저는 파리에서 가방을 샀어요

Cultural Context

문화적 맥락

Korean is deeply contextual. The same phrase changes meaning by situation. I teach you to read the room.

7 levels of "Thank you"

고마워friends
감사해요polite
감사합니다formal
감사드립니다very formal
