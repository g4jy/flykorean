Origin Story

Why I Teach Korean

In March 2025, I walked away from my business. I was completely burnt out. For the first time in my life, I just started traveling. As I wandered through different countries, I met incredible people who were genuinely curious about Korea.

> "Why do Koreans always ask about age?"

> "Why does the verb come at the very end?"

> "Why do you write addresses backwards?"

Before teaching Korean, I ran a self-directed learning coaching center in Korea for four years. At my peak, I was ranked 170th out of 450,000 tutors — the top 0.03%.

"I didn't get there because I'm smarter.
I got there because I built systems."

The philosophy behind flykorean

My goal is for you to eventually not need me. A great tutor makes themselves unnecessary. I build the system so you can fly on your own.

Philosophy

Three Pillars of Mastery

Pronunciation First

Even with 10 words, correct pronunciation makes Koreans understand you. Korean is forgiving on grammar but unforgiving on sounds. We start here.

가 Plain — soft level 1
카 Aspirated — air level 2
까 Tensed — tight level 3

Sentence Logic

Understand why Korean works the way it does. Create your own sentences instead of repeating textbook phrases.

// English: action first

I bought a bag in Paris

// Korean: context first

저는 파리에서 가방을 샀어요

Cultural Context

The same phrase can be polite or rude. I teach you to read the room, not just the textbook. 7 levels of formality.

고마워 friends
감사해요 polite
감사합니다 formal
감사드립니다 very formal
